The Vision

Why this exists.

In India, buying a home means trusting a broker — someone whose income depends on inflating the price. Most homebuyers have no way to verify if what they're paying is fair.

This tool changes that. Enter your property's locality, size, and configuration — get an instant, data-driven price benchmark based on 9,000+ real Bengaluru transactions. If a broker quotes significantly higher, you now have ground to negotiate.

Built with a Random Forest model. No fluff, no broker bias — just numbers.
